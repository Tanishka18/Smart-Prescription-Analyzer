Detailed Interaction Information

Lisinopril + Potassium: Severe Interaction

Lisinopril can increase potassium levels in the body. Taking a potassium supplement while on Lisinopril may lead to dangerously high potassium levels (hyperkalemia). This can cause symptoms like muscle weakness, abnormal heart rhythms, and in severe cases, cardiac arrest.

Lisinopril + Aspirin: Minor Interaction

The combination may slightly reduce the blood pressure-lowering effect of Lisinopril. Regular low-dose aspirin (81mg) typically has minimal impact, but higher doses may be more significant. Monitor blood pressure regularly.

Alerts & Warnings

Hyperkalemia Risk

Lisinopril and Potassium supplements may increase risk of hyperkalemia (high potassium levels). Symptoms include muscle weakness, fatigue, numbness, and cardiac arrhythmias. Consider discontinuing the potassium supplement or monitoring potassium levels closely.

Simvastatin Timing Note

Simvastatin is most effective when taken at night or before bedtime, as cholesterol production in the liver is highest overnight. Confirm that you're taking this medication in the evening as prescribed.

Recommendations

  1. Consult with your healthcare provider about the potassium supplement

    Due to the severe interaction risk with Lisinopril, discuss with your doctor whether the potassium supplement is necessary. They may recommend discontinuing it or adjusting the dosage.

  2. Regular blood tests

    Schedule regular blood tests to monitor your potassium levels, kidney function, and cholesterol levels while on this medication regimen.

  3. Blood pressure monitoring

    Monitor your blood pressure regularly at home to ensure the Lisinopril is working effectively, especially given the minor interaction with aspirin.

  4. Timing adjustments

    Ensure you're taking Simvastatin at night for maximum effectiveness, and consider spacing out the timing of your medications throughout the day.

  5. Stay hydrated

    Maintain adequate hydration, especially while taking Lisinopril, to support kidney function and prevent dehydration.
